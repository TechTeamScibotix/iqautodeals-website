## 4. How We Protect Your Information

Because the Internet is not 100% secure, we cannot guarantee that your use of our Sites and Services will be completely safe. However, we use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your Personal Information, including:

- **Password Protection:** All passwords are securely hashed using bcrypt before storage. We never store passwords in plaintext.
- **Secure Transmission:** All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
- **Database Security:** Our database connections use SSL/TLS encryption, and we use a cloud-hosted PostgreSQL database with industry-standard security measures.
- **Access Controls:** We limit access to Personal Information to employees, contractors, and agents who need that information to process it for us.

We also encourage you to use caution when using the Internet. This includes not sharing your passwords. We may link to other sites or outside party services on platforms we do not control. If you click on a link to an outside party site, you will be taken to websites or platforms we do not control. We are not responsible for their practices for protecting your Personal Information.
