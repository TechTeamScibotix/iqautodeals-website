# CBT News Submission Guide

**Publication:** CBT News (Car Biz Today)
**Website:** https://www.cbtnews.com/contribute/
**Type:** Dealer Trade Publication
**Priority:** HIGH - Reaches dealership professionals

---

## How to Submit

1. Visit: https://www.cbtnews.com/contribute/
2. Look for submission form or email on that page
3. Submit original article with requirements below

---

## Requirements Checklist

- [ ] Original article (not published elsewhere)
- [ ] Suggested headline included
- [ ] Minimum 2 supporting images
- [ ] All facts verified
- [ ] Flag uncertain facts at beginning of article

---

## Writing Guidelines

- Develop personal voice, substance over attitude
- Clearly distinguish opinions from facts
- Use italics for titles of recordings, videos, books, films, TV programs

---

## Rights & Publication

- CBT retains rights to publish/edit across cbtnews.com and social platforms
- Rolling publication basis (no strict deadline)
- Articles may be edited and published at any time
