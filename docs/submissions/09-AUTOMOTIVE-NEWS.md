# Automotive News Submission Guide

**Publication:** Automotive News
**Website:** https://www.autonews.com/
**Type:** Premier Automotive Industry Publication
**Priority:** HIGH (but difficult) - Top-tier industry credibility

---

## About Automotive News

- Premier source of industry news for automotive decision-makers
- Focus: North American automotive industry
- Covers: Techniques, strategies, business news, sales trends
- Audience: Industry executives, dealers, manufacturers, suppliers

---

## How to Submit

Automotive News is a premium publication with strict editorial standards. Options:

**Option 1: Press Release**
- Submit newsworthy announcements
- Contact their press/media team

**Option 2: Opinion/Commentary**
- Pitch thought leadership pieces
- Must have strong industry credentials

**Option 3: Story Pitch**
- Pitch yourself as a source for stories they're writing
- Offer data, insights, or expert commentary

**Contact Options:**
- Look for "Contact" or "About Us" on autonews.com
- Find editors on LinkedIn
- Email format typically: firstname.lastname@autonews.com

---

## Recommended Approach for IQ Auto Deals

Since Automotive News is highly selective, consider:

**Phase 1: Build Credibility First**
1. Get published in other trade publications (CBT News, Digital Dealer)
2. Build a portfolio of industry thought leadership
3. Gather impressive statistics (users, transactions, savings)

**Phase 2: News Angle**
- Wait for a newsworthy milestone:
  - Major funding round
  - Significant dealer partnerships
  - Impressive growth numbers
  - Industry award or recognition

**Phase 3: Expert Source**
- Position yourself as an expert source on marketplace trends
- Reach out to reporters covering digital retail, used cars, or dealer technology
- Offer to provide commentary on industry stories
