## Requirements Checklist

- [ ] Minimum 1,000 words (1,000-1,500 preferred)
- [ ] 100% original content
- [ ] NO AI-generated content
- [ ] NO copied, reworded, or promotional content
- [ ] Clear, simple American English
- [ ] Organized with H2, H3, H4 headings
- [ ] Short paragraphs
- [ ] Bullet points where appropriate
- [ ] Minimal bold text
- [ ] Keywords integrated naturally (no stuffing)
- [ ] Short, keyword-rich title (no clickbait)
- [ ] Only trusted source links (no affiliate/promo links)
- [ ] Author bio: 50-100 words, third person
- [ ] Optional: Headshot and one relevant link
