## Submission Priority Order

### Phase 1: Dealer Publications (Do First)
These reach your target audience - dealership owners and managers who might sign up as dealers.

1. **CBT News** - Easiest entry, good credibility
2. **Used Car Dealer (NIADA)** - Direct editor contact, perfect audience
3. **Digital Dealer** - Technology focus aligns well
4. **Dealer Marketing Magazine** - Marketing angle

### Phase 2: Consumer Blogs (SEO & Backlinks)
These help with search rankings and reach car buyers.

5. **MotorCraz** - Clear guidelines, good domain
6. **BusinessFirms** - Tech/AI angle
7. **AutoMotorBlog** - Broad automotive content
8. **CarzTuning** - Niche, only if angle fits

### Phase 3: Premier Publications (Long-term)
Build credibility first, then pursue:

9. **Automotive News** - Requires newsworthy milestones

## Article Templates

### For Dealer Publications (B2B)
Focus on: ROI, lead quality, reaching new markets, technology adoption

**Template Topics:**
- "How to Reach Nationwide Buyers Without Increasing Ad Spend"
- "The Rise of Dealer Competition Platforms: What You Need to Know"

## Tips for Success

1. **Personalize every pitch** - Reference their recent articles
2. **Follow guidelines exactly** - Word counts, formatting, etc.
3. **Be patient** - Most respond within 48 hours to 2 weeks
4. **Follow up once** - If no response after 1 week
5. **Accept edits gracefully** - Editors know their audience
6. **Promote published articles** - Share on social, link from your site
7. **Build relationships** - Offer to write more after first success
