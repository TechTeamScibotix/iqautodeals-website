## What Are "Bucket Brigades"?

Transitional phrases that keep readers engaged:
- "Here's the deal..."
- "But wait, there's more..."
- "Here's why this matters..."
- "The bottom line?"
- "What does this mean for you?"

Use these throughout the article to maintain reader interest.
